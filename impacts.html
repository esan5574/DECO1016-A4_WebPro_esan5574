OUR IMPACT

What We Do

Partnering with local, poverty-stricken communities in Uganda, our mission is to promote the spiritual, economic, physical and emotional development of orphaned and underprivileged children. This approach will enable generations of African children to have a future and a hope through educational development.

Hope Village

100% hOPE owns 11.5 acres of land in Mityana and has built Hope Village which includes a Primary School, Church, Girls Home, Boys Home, Babies Home and Sports Field.

Hope School

With the opening of the primary school resulting from your help, we are able to provide quality education to over 130 children.
